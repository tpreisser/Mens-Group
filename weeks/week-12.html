Discussion Questions

  • Moses' first response was "Who am I?" When have you been asked to step up and felt completely unqualified?
  • God's answer to "Who am I?" was simply "I will be with you." Why wasn't God interested in listing Moses' qualifications?
  • Moses had multiple objections—identity, authority, credibility, ability. Which of those do you tend to hide behind?
  • Moses finally just said "Please send someone else." When have you tried to talk your way out of something you knew you were supposed to do?
  • Moses was 80 years old and had been a shepherd for 40 years when God called him. What does that say about the timeline of being ready?
