Week 10

Faithful When No One's Giving You Credit

Listen

0:00 / 0:00

Reading

1 Samuel 24:1-22

Discussion Questions

  1. David had the opportunity to take what was rightfully his by force. When have you been tempted to take a shortcut to something you knew was eventually coming?
  2. David felt convicted just for cutting Saul's robe—even though Saul was trying to kill him. What does that level of integrity reveal about how David saw authority?
  3. Saul wept, acknowledged David was right, and then went back to hunting him later. When has doing the right thing not changed your circumstances?
  4. David had been anointed as a teenager but didn't become king until he was 30. What does it take to stay faithful when the timeline doesn't make sense?
  5. "I won't touch the Lord's anointed." What boundaries have you set for yourself even when breaking them would be easier?
