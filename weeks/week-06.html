Week 6

Who's Actually in Your Corner

Listen

0:00 / 0:00

Reading

1 Samuel 18:1-4, 1 Samuel 20:1-42

Discussion Questions

  1. Jonathan gave David his robe, armor, and weapons—the symbols of his own position. What does it cost to genuinely celebrate someone else's success?
  2. Jonathan was caught between loyalty to his father and loyalty to his friend. When have you been stuck between competing loyalties?
  3. Saul told Jonathan that David was a threat to Jonathan's future. Jonathan didn't buy it. How do you recognize when someone is trying to turn you against a person you should trust?
  4. Their friendship required secret meetings, signals, and risk. Real loyalty sometimes isn't convenient. What has genuine friendship cost you?
  5. Do you have someone who would sacrifice something for you? Are you that kind of friend to anyone?
