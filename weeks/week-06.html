Discussion Questions

  • Jonathan gave David his robe, armor, and weapons—the symbols of his own position. What does it cost to genuinely celebrate someone else's success?
  • Jonathan was caught between loyalty to his father and loyalty to his friend. When have you been stuck between competing loyalties?
  • Saul told Jonathan that David was a threat to Jonathan's future. Jonathan didn't buy it. How do you recognize when someone is trying to turn you against a person you should trust?
  • Their friendship required secret meetings, signals, and risk. Real loyalty sometimes isn't convenient. What has genuine friendship cost you?
  • Do you have someone who would sacrifice something for you? Are you that kind of friend to anyone?
