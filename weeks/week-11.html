Week 11

What Winning Sometimes Costs

Listen

0:00 / 0:00

Reading

Ecclesiastes 1:1-18, Ecclesiastes 2:1-26

Discussion Questions

  1. Solomon held nothing back—"whatever my eyes desired, I did not keep from them." And he still called it meaningless. What are you chasing that you assume will satisfy you?
  2. He says he "hated life" even though he achieved everything. What does that tell you about the relationship between success and fulfillment?
  3. Solomon writes that the wise man dies just like the fool—death levels everything. How does ignoring mortality affect the way men pursue things?
  4. "All is vanity and a striving after wind." Have you ever achieved something and felt the emptiness of it? What was that like?
  5. The book isn't hopeless—it ends with "Fear God and keep his commandments." What do you think Solomon learned about what actually lasts?
