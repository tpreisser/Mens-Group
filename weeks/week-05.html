Questions

  • Sanballat and Tobiah mocked the work before it even got started. Who or what has tried to discourage you before you could even begin?
  • Nehemiah's response to mockery was "so we built the wall." He didn't argue, he worked. When is engaging with critics the wrong move?
  • They built with a tool in one hand and a weapon in the other. What does it look like to build while staying guarded against real threats?
  • The enemies tried to get Nehemiah to "come down" for a meeting—to pull him off the work with something that seemed reasonable. What distractions disguise themselves as opportunities in your life?
  • "I am doing a great work and I cannot come down." What would you need to believe about what you're building to say that?
