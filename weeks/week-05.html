Week 5

Building While They Mock

Listen

0:00 / 0:00

Reading

Nehemiah 4:1-23, Nehemiah 6:1-9

Discussion Questions

  1. Sanballat and Tobiah mocked the work before it even got started. Who or what has tried to discourage you before you could even begin?
  2. Nehemiah's response to mockery was "so we built the wall." He didn't argue, he worked. When is engaging with critics the wrong move?
  3. They built with a tool in one hand and a weapon in the other. What does it look like to build while staying guarded against real threats?
  4. The enemies tried to get Nehemiah to "come down" for a meeting—to pull him off the work with something that seemed reasonable. What distractions disguise themselves as opportunities in your life?
  5. "I am doing a great work and I cannot come down." What would you need to believe about what you're building to say that?
