Discussion Questions

  1. Joseph did everything right and still ended up in prison for years. When has obedience not led to the outcome you expected?
  2. The cupbearer forgot Joseph for two full years. What does it feel like to be forgotten by someone who could've helped you?
  3. In every situation—slavery, prison—Joseph was faithful and rose to responsibility. What does it look like to be excellent in a season that feels like it doesn't matter?
  4. Joseph was 17 when he was sold and 30 when he stood before Pharaoh. How do you keep going when the timeline doesn't match your expectations?
  5. What's your "chapter that looks like nothing"? What would faithfulness look like in it right now?
