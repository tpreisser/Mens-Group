Reading

1 Kings 19:1-18

Discussion Questions

  1. What changes in Elijah between the end of chapter 18 and the start of chapter 19?
  2. Why did one threat from Jezebel undo him after he just faced down 450 men?
  3. God's first response is food and sleep—not a rebuke. What does that tell us about how God designed us?
  4. Elijah said "I alone am left" but 7,000 faithful remained. When you're going through something hard, does your mind go to worst-case scenarios that aren't actually true?
  5. Have you experienced a crash that came after a win, not before it?
  6. God's next step for Elijah was practical tasks and someone to share the load. When you're depleted, what actually helps you get moving again?
