Week 7

What Actually Sets You Off

Listen

0:00 / 0:00

Reading

Jonah 3:10-4:11

Discussion Questions

  1. Jonah was angry that his enemies received mercy. When have you been frustrated that someone got grace you didn't think they deserved?
  2. God asks Jonah twice: "Do you do well to be angry?" What would it look like to honestly ask yourself that question when you're heated?
  3. Jonah cared more about a plant that gave him shade than about 120,000 people. What does that reveal about how comfort can distort our priorities?
  4. The book ends with God's question unanswered. Why do you think the story ends without resolution?
  5. What's something that sets you off that might be revealing something deeper underneath?
